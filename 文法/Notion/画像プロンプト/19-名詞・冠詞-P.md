### 4. Section C: Rules & Comparison (Center/Right Middle)
- **Table/Chart**: Comparison between 可算名詞 vs 不可算名詞 and a/an vs the
- **Content**:
  - **可算名詞 vs 不可算名詞**:
    - 可算名詞: a/an ○, 複数形 ○ (例: a book, two books)
    - 不可算名詞: a/an ×, 複数形 × (例: water, some water)
  - **冠詞の使い分け**:
    - a/an: 可算名詞の単数形のみ、不特定
    - the: 可算・不可算すべてOK、特定されたもの
  - **所有格の作り方**:
    - 人・生物: 's を使う (Tom's book)
    - 無生物: of を使う (the leg of the desk)
    - 例外: 時・距離は 's OK (today's news, ten minutes' walk)
  - **無冠詞になる場合**:
    - 食事: have breakfast/lunch/dinner
    - スポーツ: play soccer/baseball
    - 本来の目的: go to school (通学), go to bed (寝る)

### 5. Section D: Important Idioms (Bottom Area)
- **List Layout**:
  - 1. **of + 抽象名詞** = 形容詞化 (of importance = important, of value = valuable, of use = useful)
  - 2. **with + 抽象名詞** = 副詞化 (with ease = easily, with care = carefully, with speed = speedily)
  - 3. **to + one's 感情名詞** = 〜なことに (to my surprise = 驚いたことに, to my joy = 嬉しいことに, to my disappointment = 残念なことに)
  - 4. **the + 形容詞** = 〜な人々 (the rich = 金持ちの人々, the young = 若者)
  - 5. **the + 姓の複数形** = 〜家 (The Smiths = スミス一家)
  - 6. **play the 楽器** (play the piano, play the guitar) ※スポーツは無冠詞
