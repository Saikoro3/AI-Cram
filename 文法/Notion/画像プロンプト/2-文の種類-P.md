### 2. Section A: Declarative & Interrogative Sentences (Left Column)

#### Declarative Sentences (平叙文)
- **Concept Visual**: A straight horizontal arrow representing straightforward statements
- **Explanation Text**:
  - "事実や意見をありのままに述べる基本的な文"
  - "肯定文: 「～である」「～する」"
  - "否定文: 「～でない」「～しない」(not の位置に注意)"

#### Interrogative Sentences (疑問文)
- **Types Diagram**: Four branches showing different question types
  - Yes/No疑問文: "文頭に助動詞・be動詞"
  - WH疑問文: "具体的情報を尋ねる (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How)"
  - 付加疑問文: "～ですよね？と確認"
  - 修辞疑問文: "答えを求めない反語表現"

- **Key Example**:
  - "What did you buy? (あなたは何を買いましたか？)"
  - "→ 疑問詞 What は文中で O(目的語)の役割"

### 3. Section B: Question Word Elements (Center Top)
- **Visual Metaphor**: A puzzle piece diagram showing how question words fit into sentence structure (S, V, O, C, M)
- **Table Content**:

| 疑問詞 | 文中の役割 | 例文 |
|-------|----------|------|
| Who | S (主語) | Who plays the piano? |
| What | O (目的語) | What did you buy? |
| When | M (修飾語) | When will he come? |

- **Keywords**: "疑問詞 = 欠けている要素の代わり"

### 4. Section C: Tag Questions & Answer Pattern (Center Middle)
- **Visual**: Two-way switch diagram (肯定⇔否定)
- **Rule Box**:
  - "前が肯定 → 後ろは否定"
  - "前が否定 → 後ろは肯定"

- **Answer Pattern (Important)**:
  - "Yes → 後ろは肯定文"
  - "No → 後ろは否定文"
  - "英語は「事実が肯定か否定か」で Yes/No を決める"

- **Example**:
  - "You are a student, aren't you?"
  - " Yes, I am. (はい、学生です)"
  - "He doesn't like natto, does he?"
  - " Yes, he does. (いいえ、好きです) ← 日本語と逆!"

### 5. Section D: Imperative Sentences (Right Column)
- **Concept Visual**: Command arrow pointing forward
- **Explanation Text**:
  - "主語 You を省略"
  - "動詞の原形で文を始める"

- **Pattern Table**:

| 構文 | 意味 | 例文 |
|-----|------|------|
| 命令文, and... | そうすれば | Study hard, and you will pass. |
| 命令文, or... | さもないと | Hurry up, or you will be late. |

### 6. Section E: Exclamatory Sentences (Bottom Area)
- **Visual**: Two pathways diverging - "What path" (with noun) and "How path" (with adjective/adverb)
- **Core Concept Box**:
  - "What = 名詞と仲良し"
  - "How = 形容詞・副詞と仲良し"

- **Formula Display**:
  - **What感嘆文**: What + (a/an) + 形容詞 + 名詞 + S + V !
    - 例: What a cute cat this is!
  
  - **How感嘆文**: How + 形容詞/副詞 + S + V !
    - 例: How beautiful this flower is!

### 7. Bottom Summary Bar
- **Key Points**:
  1. "疑問詞は文中で S, O, C, M の役割を持つ"
  2. "付加疑問文は「肯定⇔否定」のスイッチ"
  3. "感嘆文は「名詞あり→What」「形容詞・副詞のみ→How」"
