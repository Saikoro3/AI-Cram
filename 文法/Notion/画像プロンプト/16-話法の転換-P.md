# Nanobananapro用プロンプト：話法の転換

## [Design Description]
Create a comprehensive educational infographic sheet about "話法の転換 (Narration / Direct and Indirect Speech)".

**Style:** Clean, professional Japanese cram school textbook style. High information density, organized grid layout. Infographic design with clear visual hierarchy.

**Color Palette:** Navy blue (#1a365d) for headers, white (#ffffff) background, yellow (#ffd700) for highlights, green (#22c55e) for direct speech examples, orange (#f97316) for indirect speech examples, red (#ef4444) for important warnings.

**Typography:** Use clear, legible fonts. Bold for headers, regular weight for body text. Ensure all Japanese and English text is highly readable.

**Overall Layout:** 16:9 aspect ratio, divided into 4 main sections with clear borders and hierarchy.

```
Ex: He said that he was busy.
   （彼は、自分は忙しいと言った）
```

### 4. Section C: 転換の4ステップ (Middle, 60% width)
**Border:** Yellow accent border (most prominent)
**Title (navy, bold):** 「B. 話法の転換（平叙文）- 4ステップ」

**Visual Elements:**
- Large numbered flowchart (1→2→3→4) with arrows
- Example sentence transformation shown step by step with highlighting

**Step-by-step diagram:**
```
【転換の探偵メソッド】誰が誰に、いつ言ったか？

例文：He said to me, "I am busy now."
      ↓↓↓↓ 4ステップで変換 ↓↓↓↓

STEP 1: 伝達動詞を変える
・say → say (相手なし)
・say to (人) → tell (人) (相手あり)
→ He told me ...

STEP 2: 接続詞でつなぐ
・引用符 " " を外す
・接続詞 that でつなぐ（省略可）
→ He told me that ...

STEP 3: 人称代名詞を変える
・発言者の「I」→「he」に
・聞き手の「you」→「I/me」に
→ ... that he ...

STEP 4: 時制と副詞句を変える
・時制の一致：am → was
・副詞句：now → then
→ ... that he was busy then.

完成：He told me that he was busy then.
```

### 6. Section E: 疑問文・命令文・感嘆文 (Bottom, full width)
**Border:** Red accent border
**Title (navy, bold):** 「C. 話法の転換（疑問文・命令文・感嘆文）」

**Layout:** Three columns side by side

**Column 1: 疑問文の転換**
```
【疑問文】伝達動詞は ask
間接疑問文の語順 (S + V) に！

❶ Yes/No疑問文
接続詞：if / whether（～かどうか）

直接：He said to me, "Do you know her?"
間接：He asked me if I knew her.
     (Do you know → I knew)

❷ 疑問詞のある疑問文
疑問詞をそのまま接続詞に

直接：He said to me, "What are you doing?"
間接：He asked me what I was doing.
     (What are you doing → what I was doing)
```

**Column 2: 命令文の転換**
```
【命令文】to不定詞を使う
tell/ask/order + O + to V

❶ 肯定命令文
tell [ask] + O + to V
(OにVするように言う[頼む])

直接：He said to me, "Open the door."
間接：He told me to open the door.

❷ 否定命令文
tell [ask] + O + not to V
(OにVしないように言う[頼む])

直接：He said to me, "Don't be late."
間接：He told me not to be late.
```

**Column 3: 感嘆文の転換**
```
【感嘆文】exclaim を使う
または平叙文に直す

直接：She said, "How beautiful 
     this flower is!"

間接❶：She exclaimed how beautiful
      that flower was.
      （感嘆文の語順のまま）

間接❷：She exclaimed that that 
      flower was very beautiful.
      （平叙文に直す）
```
