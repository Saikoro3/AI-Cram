**[Visual Structure & Text Content]**
Please render the following text and graphics precisely based on the grid layout.

### 1. Header Area
- **Main Title**: "第14章 比較 完全解説"
- **Subtitle**: "The Complete Guide to Comparison"
- **Icon**: Three ascending bars (short, medium, tall) or balance scale symbol representing comparison levels

### 2. Section A: Three Forms of Comparison (Left Column)
- **Concept Visual**: Three-level diagram showing 原級 → 比較級 → 最上級 progression with arrows
- **Explanation Text**:
  - "**原級**: as ... as (同じくらい～)"
  - "**比較級**: -er / more ... than (～より…)"
  - "**最上級**: -est / most ... (一番…)"
- **Example Sentences**:
  - "A is **as tall as** B. (AはBと同じくらい背が高い)"
  - "A is **taller than** B. (AはBより背が高い)"
  - "A is **the tallest** in his class. (Aはクラスで一番背が高い)"

### 3. Section B: Formation Rules (Center Top)
- **Visual Metaphor**: Transformation flowchart showing old → older → oldest
- **Table/Chart**: Change rules
  - **基本**: old → older → oldest
  - **-eで終わる**: large → larger → largest
  - **短母音+子音**: big → bigger → biggest
  - **子音+y**: happy → happier → happiest
  - **長い語**: beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful
- **Keywords**: "規則変化 / 不規則変化"

### 4. Section C: Irregular Forms (Center Middle)
- **Table/Chart**: Irregular comparison forms
- **Content**:
  - good/well → better → best
  - bad/ill → worse → worst
  - many/much → more → most
  - little → less → least
- **Visual**: Star icon marking these as "must-memorize" items

### 5. Section D: Key Constructions (Right Column)
- **Important Patterns**:
  - "**The + 比較級 ..., the + 比較級 ~**: 比例関係"
    - The more you explain, the worse it sounds.
  - "**比較級 + than any other + 単数**: 他のどれよりも"
  - "**one of the + 最上級 + 複数名詞**: 最も～なものの一つ"
  - "**the + 比較級 + of the two**: 二つの中でより～な方"

### 6. Section E: No More Than Expressions (Bottom Area)
- **List Layout with Visual Diagram**:
  - **no more than**: たった～しか (only) 【少ない！不満】
    - He has **no more than** 1,000 yen. (たった千円しか)
  - **no less than**: ～も (as many as) 【多い！驚き】
    - He has **no less than** 1,000 yen. (千円も)
  - **not more than**: せいぜい (at most)
  - **not less than**: 少なくとも (at least)
- **Special Note**: クジラの構文
  - A whale is **no more a fish** than a horse is. (馬が魚でないのと同様に、クジラも魚ではない)

### 7. Section F: Core Strategy Box (Bottom Right)
- **強調表現**:
  - 比較級の強調: much, far, a lot, even, still (**very不可**)
  - 最上級の強調: by far, much, the very
- **倍数表現**: times as ~ as (twice as ~ as)
