### 1. Header Area

- **Subtitle**: "The Complete Guide to Passive Voice"

### 2. Section A: Basic Concept (Left Column)
- **Concept Visual**: Diagram showing the transformation from active to passive:
  - Active: S → V → O (arrow flowing right)
  - Passive: S(元O) ← be+p.p. ← by 元S (arrow flowing left with reversal)
- **Explanation Text**:
  - "**受動態の基本公式**: 主語(S) + be動詞 + 過去分詞(p.p.) + (by 動作主)"
  - "視点の転換：「誰が何をどうした」から「何が誰によってどうされた」へ"
  - "**受動態にできるのは原則として他動詞だけ**"
- **Example Sentence**:
  - "能動態: Many people **love** this song."
  - "受動態: This song **is loved** by many people."

### 3. Section B: Core Image & Nuance (Center/Right Top)
- **Visual Metaphor**: A spotlight shining on an object (representing the shift of focus from the doer to the receiver of the action)
- **Keywords**: "**視点の転換**, **動作の受け手に焦点**, **他動詞のみ**"
- **Time Expression Table**:
  - 現在形: is/are + p.p.
  - 過去形: was/were + p.p.
  - 未来形: will be + p.p.
  - 完了形: have been + p.p.
  - 助動詞: 助動詞 + be + p.p.

### 4. Section C: Rules & Comparison (Center/Right Middle)
- **Table/Chart**: Comparison between sentence patterns in passive voice
- **Content**:
  - **SVOO型の受動態**:
    - 人を主語: I **was given** this ring.
    - 物を主語: This ring **was given to** me.
    - ⚠️ buy型(for型)は人を主語にできない
  - **SVOC型の受動態**:
    - She **is called** Helen. (S=Cの関係が維持)
    - 使役動詞・知覚動詞: to が復活
    - She **was made to open** the bag.
  - **群動詞の受動態**:
    - I **was spoken to** by a stranger.
    - 前置詞は省略不可、セットで扱う

- **List Layout**:

  - 6. **be caught in** 〜 : (雨など)にあう

### 6. Section E: Exceptions (Right Bottom)
- **Alert Box** (Warning style):
  - "**受動態にできない自動詞**"
  - die, happen, appear, disappear, arrive, exist
  - ❌ The accident **was happened**. (誤り)
  - ✅ The accident **happened**. (正しい)
