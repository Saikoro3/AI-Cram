### 1. Header Area
- **Main Title**: "第10章 分詞構文 完全解説"
- **Subtitle**: "The Complete Guide to Participial Construction"
- **Icon**: Compression symbol (圧縮アイコン) showing a long sentence being compressed into shorter form, or transformation arrows

### 2. Section A: Basic Concept (Left Column)
- **Concept Visual**: Diagram showing transformation from "接続詞 + 主語 + 動詞" to "分詞(-ing)" with arrow indicating compression/simplification
- **Explanation Text**:
  - "分詞構文 = 接続詞を使った長い文を、分詞を使って短くスッキリさせたもの"
  - "分詞が「副詞」の働きをして文全体を修飾する"
  - **作り方の3ステップ**:
    1. 接続詞をとる
    2. 主語が主節と同じならとる
    3. 動詞を現在分詞(-ing)にする
- **Example Sentence**:
  - **Before**: "**When I walked** along the street, I met him."
  - **After**: "**Walking** along the street, I met him."
  - (通りを歩いているとき、私は彼に会った。)

### 3. Section B: Core Image & Nuance (Center Top)
- **Visual Metaphor**: Six icons representing the 6 meanings with visual symbols (clock for 時, lightbulb for 理由, barrier for 譲歩, fork in road for 条件, parallel lines for 付帯状況, arrow sequence for 結果)
- **Keywords**: "文脈判断 | 6つの意味 | 接続詞省略"
- **6つの意味 Table**:
  - ① 時 (when) : Walking along the street, I met him.
  - ② 理由 (because) : Feeling tired, I went to bed early.
  - ③ 譲歩 (though) : Admitting what you say, I still think you are wrong.
  - ④ 条件 (if) : Turning to the right, you will find the station.
  - ⑤ 付帯状況 (while) : He sat in the chair, reading a book.
  - ⑥ 結果 (and) : He fell, breaking his leg.

### 4. Section C: Advanced Forms (Center/Right Middle)
- **Chart Title**: "応用形3パターン"
- **Content**:
  - **否定形**: **Not** -ing (Notを分詞の直前に置く)
    - "Not knowing what to say, I remained silent."
  - **完了形**: **Having** + 過去分詞 (主節より前の時を表す)
    - "Having finished my homework, I went out for a walk."
  - **受動態**: **(Being)** + 過去分詞 (Beingは省略可)
    - "(Being) Written in simple English, this book is easy to read."

### 5. Section D: Special Constructions (Right Column)
- **Title**: "独立分詞構文 & with構文"
- **Content**:
  - **独立分詞構文**: 主語が主節と異なる場合は主語を残す
    - "**The sun having set**, we went home." (日が沈んだので、私たちは帰った。)
  - **with + O + C**: O is C の関係が成立
    - with + O + **-ing** (能動) : "with his eyes shining"
    - with + O + **-p.p.** (受動) : "with his legs crossed"

- **List Layout**:

  - 4. **Judging from ...** : ～から判断すると
